About Us

We realised that there is a wide gap between what was being taught to us in our colleges and what was expected from us in the tech industry. The IT Industry is moving at such a fast pace, and there is a growing need for skilled minds, which is currently lacking in India. We came together with a vision to fulfill this need, and thus, The Semicolon Initiative was conceived. Our goal is to make programming more accessible to students and fresh graduates, and we do so with the help of offline bootcamps that we have curated in liason with various industry leaders.

Learn the Best and the Latest Technologies

An amateur programmer spends a lot of time in deciding what to learn first, we have curated a course which includes the most in demand programming stack so that no effort is wasted.

Classroom Setting over Online Learning

Online courses lack the personal touch that the classroom setting provides, group dynamics are less favorable too in online eduaction hence we went with offline bootcamps conducted on weekends to maximize learning.

A Push into the Industry

The biggest career mistakes are often the first, hence we provide career guidance, internship and placement opprtunities as well as mentorship programs to help build a great profile and to ace interviews.
