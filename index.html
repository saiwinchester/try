About

_____

We founded Semicolon after noticing the gap that exists between what is taught to us, specifically in our colleges, and what is required from us in the industry as developers. We firmly believe in the fast moving pace of our Tech Industry and acknowledge the fact that it takes young creative minds to make an impact on the same. All we want to do, is to make Full Stack Development more accessable to students, with interactive courses we have developed ourselves.

COURSES OFFERED

_____

The whole premise of Full Stack Development is set on the idea that a developer who can proficiently build all fronts of an Application, Web or ortherwise, has a reduced need of dependncy on specialized fronts. Therefore, we at Semicolon offer a wide variety of courses that aim at making a programmer handle all kinds of work in our Industry.
